Tip 2 Secure Attachment

According to John Bowlby and Mary Ainsworth, infants have various levels of attachment. Mental disorders apart, the level of attachment of your infant will most of the time depends on how you raise them!

   -Do not abuse your infant, as this will cause them to go down the level heirarchy,
   namely to the "Insecure Resistant/Ambvient" stage, the worse of them all.
   -Treat your child well so they can succeed! Children who were securely attached usually
   have higher self esteem, better romantic relationships, higher levels of education.
