Tip 3 Language Learning

Once your infant starts babbling, do not wait to teach them words! At ages one through six, babies reach a sensetive period for language. Teaching them new words and not encouraging engagement with others can lead to language learning problems later on in life.

   -The older you become, the harder it is to learn a second language.
    With that in mind, raising your child to be bilingual is highly encouraged!
   -A healthy diet during infancy will influence your child to eat healthy overall in their
    later life, reducing the risk of strokes, which can permanently damage speaking abilities!
